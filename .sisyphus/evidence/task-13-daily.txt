Task 13 daily reset implementation evidence

- Daily counting is computed at query time with UTC day boundaries:
  - day start: YYYY-MM-DDT00:00:00.000Z
  - next day start: +24h
- no-response count queries filter with:
  - reviewed_at >= dayStartUtc
  - reviewed_at < nextDayStartUtc
- This naturally resets no-response caps at UTC midnight without cron/jobs.
- Deferred-card query includes:
  - cards deferred in the future with no_response activity today
  - cards marked await-session by due_at == nextDayStartUtc
